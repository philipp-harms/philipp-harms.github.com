Philipp Harms

Mathematician

Bio

Philipp did his PhD in mathematics at the University of Vienna in shape analysis and comparison. Eager to apply his analytical and mathematically trained mind to a field meeting his passion for social justice, he then chose to join the education research and development laboratory Harvard EdLabs. He is actively engaged in the theoretical and applied mathematical research, building formal models that relate directly to EdLabs' innovations. Philipp also contributes his unique perspective to EdLabs' experimental designs.

Specialties: shape analysis, differential geometry in infinite dimensions, stochastic processes, micro- and behavioral economics, mathematical modeling, education.
